I am a **postdoctoral researcher in physical oceanography** at [North Carolina State University](https://www.ncsu.edu/), interested in **multi-scale ocean dynamics**, **air-sea-ice interactions**, and **cross-shelf water exchange**. I use and develop numerical ocean models (ROMS, FVCOM), combined with data assimilation and machine learning, to study circulation across coastal, shelf, and open-ocean systems.

At NCSU I work on **eddies in the Gulf of Mexico** and **water mass transformation in the Caribbean Sea**, building on earlier work on estuarine and shelf circulation in Australia and China.
