I am a **research scientist in physical oceanography** at [North Carolina State University](https://www.ncsu.edu/), interested in **regional-coastal-estuarine ocean circulation dynamics**, **air-sea-ice interactions**, and **hybrid ocean forecasting**. I use and develop numerical ocean models (ROMS, FVCOM), combined with data assimilation and machine learning, to study currents and circulation across coastal, shelf, and open-ocean systems.

At NCSU I work on **loop current (eddies) in the Gulf of Mexico** and **water mass transformation in the Caribbean Sea**, building on earlier work on estuarine and shelf circulation in Australia and China.
