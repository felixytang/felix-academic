About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Physical Oceanography at North Carolina State University. My research focuses on multi-scale ocean dynamics, air-sea-ice interactions, and cross-shelf water exchange. I employ numerical ocean models (ROMS, FVCOM) combined with data assimilation techniques and ML/AI approaches to investigate ocean circulation and climate dynamics.

Research Interests

Ocean Dynamics

Multi-scale physical oceanography and circulation

Air-Sea-Ice

Interactions and cross-shelf water exchange

Numerical Modeling

ROMS, FVCOM, and data assimilation

ML/AI

Machine learning for ocean science

Current Research

At NCSU, I am conducting advanced research with a focus on:

  • Data assimilation techniques to enhance ocean model accuracy
  • Eddies in the Gulf of Mexico using numerical models and ML/AI
  • Water mass transformation in the Caribbean Sea
